Kenneth A. Pickar
Kenneth A. Pickar

Ken Pickar is a Visiting Professor of Mechanical Engineering (1998-present), and J.Stanley Johnson Professor (1999-2003) at the California Institute of Technology, Pasadena, CA. He has taught courses in Entrepreneurship, Management of Technology, Product Design for the Developing World and Design for Freedom from Disability He won a 2005 Award for Excellence in Teaching by the undergraduate students of Caltech. He has also taught at UCLA Anderson and USC Marshall.

His public corporate board positions include, Level One Corporation (1997-1999) NASDAQ, then sold to Intel in 1999; Neustar Corporation (2000- ). The company went IPO on the NYSE in June 2005; Ness Technologies which is listed on the NASDAQ. He is on the Audit Committees of Neustar and Ness and a board position with H2Scan Corporation (2005-10). He has been on the Operating Committee of Idealab (2004-2006).

Since 1999, he has been a member of the Tech Coast Angels (LA) and has made early-stage investments through this Southern California Network. He is on the Executive Committee of the LA network.

Between 1993-1998, he was a Senior Vice President, Engineering and Technology AlliedSignal Aerospace, Torrance, CA (now Honeywell. Between 1983-1992, he was Manager-Electronic Systems Research Center at the GE Corporate Research and Development, Schenectady, New York. Between 1974-1982, he held other management positions at Signetics, Bell Northern Research and between 1966-1974, he was a member of the Technical Staff, Bell Laboratories, Murray Hill, New Jersey.

His education was at the Bronx High School of Science, Queens College, City University of New York, BS, cum laude, Phi Beta Kappa, Physics and Math and at The University of Pennsylvania - 9/61-10/66 - MS, PhD – Physics with a thesis: “Third Sound in Liquid Helium”. He has taken prior Management courses in Management of Research - Harvard Business School, Financial Management for Non-Financial Managers - Wharton School University of Pennsylvania, GE Executive Development Course - Crotonville, NY - Baldridge Examiner Training, and Six Sigma training 1995.

Ken was a member of the Board of Directors (2005 - 2010) of the LA Regional Food Bank. He enjoys horticulture, hiking, skiing, biking, music and books. He is married with three children and six grandchildren.
